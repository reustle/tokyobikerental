  • 4 stations in Taito Ward, Tokyo
  • ¥200 for the first 4 hours
  • ¥300 for the entire day (same-day return)
  • ¥600 for 3 days
  • ¥1,200 for 7 days
  • Official Website (no English)

Taito Ward in Tokyo has a simple bike share program where you can rent and return bicycles at 4 separate locations (mapped below). It is unlikely they speak English, but you can likely manage using Google Translate.

  • Asakusa Station (east)
  • Shin-Okachimachi Station
  • Naka-Okachimachi Station
  • Asakusa Station (west)
